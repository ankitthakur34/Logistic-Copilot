# Bill of Lading (B/L)

## Overview

A Bill of Lading (B/L) is one of the most important shipping documents in international cargo transportation.

It serves three purposes:

- Receipt of goods
- Contract of carriage
- Document of title

## Information Included

- Shipper
- Consignee
- Notify Party
- Vessel Name
- Voyage Number
- Port of Loading
- Port of Discharge
- Container Numbers
- Cargo Description

## Types

- Original Bill of Lading
- Sea Waybill
- Express Release
- House Bill of Lading
- Master Bill of Lading

## Why it matters

Cargo cannot normally be released until the Bill of Lading requirements are satisfied.
