# Container Types

## Dry Container

Used for standard cargo such as electronics, furniture and machinery.

Common Sizes

- 20 FT
- 40 FT
- 40 FT High Cube

---

## Reefer Container

Temperature controlled container used for

- Food
- Medicine
- Chemicals

Typical Temperature Range

-30°C to +30°C

---

## Open Top Container

Used for oversized cargo loaded from the top.

---

## Flat Rack

Used for heavy machinery and industrial equipment.

---

## Tank Container

Used for transporting liquid chemicals and food-grade liquids.
