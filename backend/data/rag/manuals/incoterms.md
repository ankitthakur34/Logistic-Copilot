# Incoterms

Incoterms define the responsibilities between buyers and sellers during international trade.

Common Incoterms

FOB

Free On Board

Seller delivers goods onto the vessel.

---

CIF

Cost Insurance Freight

Seller pays freight and insurance.

---

EXW

Ex Works

Buyer assumes almost all transportation responsibilities.

---

DAP

Delivered At Place

Seller delivers cargo to destination.
