# Weather Delay Incident Report

## Summary

Shipment SHP0001 experienced a significant delay while approaching the Port of Rotterdam due to severe weather conditions in the North Sea.

## Timeline

04:30 UTC
- Weather warning received from Rotterdam Port Authority.

05:10 UTC
- Wind speed exceeded safe operating limits.

06:00 UTC
- Vessel speed reduced.

08:20 UTC
- Port suspended unloading operations.

11:30 UTC
- Customer notified regarding possible delay.

## Impact

- ETA delayed by approximately 48 hours.
- Containers remained secure.
- No cargo damage reported.
- Crew safety maintained.

## Root Cause

Extreme weather conditions caused temporary closure of terminal operations.

## Corrective Actions

- Updated shipment ETA.
- Customer informed.
- Operations team monitored weather every six hours.
- Port slot rebooked after weather improved.

## Current Status

Weather conditions are improving.

Estimated unloading will resume after port authorities reopen terminal operations.
