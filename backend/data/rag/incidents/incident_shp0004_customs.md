# Customs Inspection Delay

## Summary

Shipment SHP0004 was selected for random customs inspection after arriving at Hamburg.

## Findings

Customs authorities requested additional documentation regarding lithium battery declarations.

## Impact

- Shipment delayed 24 hours.
- Customer informed.
- Containers remained at customs terminal.

## Resolution

Operations team submitted missing documentation.

Customs released shipment after successful verification.

## Lessons Learned

Future shipments containing lithium batteries should include complete declaration documents before vessel departure.
